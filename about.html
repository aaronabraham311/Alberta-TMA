About Us

History

On December 9th, 2017, the Calgary Junior High Team Math Attack (CJHTMA) held their inaugural event at the University of Calgary with the help of Dr. Joseph Ling and Dr. Robert Woodrow from the University of Calgaryâ€™s Department of Mathematics and Statistics. It hosted 69 students, whose grades ranged from 6 to 9.

The Edmonton Junior High Team Math Contest (EJHTMC) was independently organized on March 17th, 2018 on a similar premise to the CJHTMA. This contest garnered over 40 participants from grades 4 to 9. Proctored by Mr. Trevor Pasanen and Dr. Sean Graves, the competition was hosted at the JAMES Math Circle at the University of Alberta.

After realizing that these two competitions pursued the same overall goal, the two leadership teams merged in June 2018 to form Alberta Team Math Attack.
